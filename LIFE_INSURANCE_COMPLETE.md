## 🎯 Key Innovations

### 1. White-Label Partnership Strategy

**Why Cathay Life?**
- 60+ years experience
- 4M+ customers globally
- AA+ credit rating (S&P)
- 98.5% claim payout rate

**UX Journey: "Hide Partner, Show Value"**

```
Step 1: Touch Pain Point (Nhu cầu)
   ↓
Step 2: Show Solution (Giải pháp)
   ↓
Step 3: Build Trust (Uy tín đối tác - Bottom of page)
```

**Placement:**
- Cathay Life logo & description: **Bottom of product page only**
- Above the fold: Focus on customer needs & benefits
- After interest: Credibility booster

**Why this works:**
- Customer focuses on THEIR needs, not brand names
- Lifestyle branding = Familiar, trustworthy
- Cathay Life badge = Credibility after interest
