# Merchant App Architecture - Lifestyle Super App

> **Tài liệu cấu trúc App Merchant** - Dành cho đối tác bán hàng
>
> Quản lý cửa hàng, sản phẩm, đơn hàng, doanh thu, marketing

---

## 📱 Platform

### Mobile/Web Apps
- **apps/mobile-merchant** - React Native app cho merchant (iOS/Android)
- **apps/merchant-web** - Web portal cho merchant (desktop-optimized)

### Backend Services
- **services/merchant-service** - Merchant profile, store management
- **services/product-service** - Product catalog, inventory
- **services/order-service** - Order processing, fulfillment
- **services/review-service** - Reviews & ratings
- **services/promotion-service** - Marketing campaigns, discounts
- **services/analytics-service** - Sales analytics, reports
- **services/chat-service** - Real-time messaging (merchant-customer-driver)

---

## 🗺️ App Structure

### Main Sections

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  🏪 Trang chủ      │  📦 Đơn hàng  │  📊 Thống kê │
│                    │               │              │
│  💰 Tài chính      │  📢 Marketing │  ⚙️ Cài đặt  │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

1. **🏪 Trang chủ** (Dashboard) - Overview & quick actions
2. **📦 Đơn hàng** (Orders) - Order management
3. **🎁 Sản phẩm** (Products) - Product management
4. **💰 Tài chính** (Finance) - Wallet & transactions
5. **📊 Thống kê** (Analytics) - Sales reports
6. **📢 Marketing** (Marketing) - Promotions & ads
7. **⚙️ Cài đặt** (Settings) - Store settings
